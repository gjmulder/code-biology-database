About this report & caveats

Two independent axes over the same papers, reported side by side — neither is ground truth. pages e = the per-chunk (8192-token "page") corpus-contrastive embedding score per criterion, max-pooled over a paper's chunks (positive = reads as arguing the criterion). verdicts are the domain-general graded judge's met / unclear / not_met per criterion (CLAUDE.md §9/§9.1), each met gated by a verbatim quote; only the ~100 re-judged papers carry them (the rest show –). Hover a verdict cell for its graded value and confidence. The verdicts are still synthetic labels from a comparatively weak judge, so ranks are more trustworthy than absolute magnitudes. The min metric is the weakest-link reading: per Barbieri a biological code requires all three criteria.
