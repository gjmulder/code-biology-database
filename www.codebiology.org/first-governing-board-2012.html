First Governing Board – 2012

The International Society of Code Biology (ISCB) has been officially constituted in Ferrara (Italy) before notary
Chiara Maria Florio on 28 November 2012. The present Governing Board is formed by the following members:

1 Marcello Barbieri president University of Ferrara, Italy
2 Jan-Hendrik Hofmeyr vice-president University of Stellenbosch, South Africa
3 Almo Farina secretary University of Urbino, Italy
4 Peter Wills treasurer University of Auckland, New Zealand
5 Stefan Artmann German National Academy of Sciences
6 Joachim De Beule Free University of Brussels, Belgium
7 Peter Dittrich Friedrich-Schiller-University Jena, Germany
8 Dennis Görlich University of Münster, Germany
9 Stefan Kühn University of Stellenbosch, South Africa
10 Chris Ottolenghi Université Paris Descartes, France
11 Liz Stillwaggon Swan Oregon State University, USA
12 Morten Tønnessen University of Stavanger, Norway
