Call for Papers

The The Twelfth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Guimarães  (Portugal)  2– 6 June 2026, under the auspices of the International Society of Code Biology and of the Institute of Computer Graphics (CCG) of the University of Minho.
The Conference will host individual talks and the General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver a talk are invited to email an Abstract of between 250-500 words to guimaraes-abstracts@codebiology.org

Abstracts must be written in formats such as .doc or .rtf – no PDF please.
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2026
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

For any question, please contact the local organizer:
João Carlos Major   joaocarlosmajor@gmail.com

Looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in Guimarães!
