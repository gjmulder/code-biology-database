The Conference takes place in the Aula Magna of the:

Collegio Raffaello

Piazza della Repubblica 13

61029 Urbino


NB - The correct address of Collegio Raffaello is: Piazza della Repubblica 13


From Porta Santa Lucia, where the new bus station is situated, take Via Bramante and at the end of it turn left in Via Raffaello that leads directly in Piazza della Repubblica.


NB – Please notice that the Collegio Raffaello is the conference place whereas the Collegio Internazionale is a hotel for students and academics. The Collegio Internazionale is situated in Piazza San Filippo 2, just a few yards away from the Collegio Raffaello.
