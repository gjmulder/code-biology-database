Call for Papers

The International Conferences in Code Biology aim at bringing together scholars and researchers who are interested in the study of all codes of life, from the genetic code to the codes of culture.

The Third International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Urbino, from 24 to 28 May 2016, under the auspices of the University of Urbino and the International Society of Code Biology.
The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver individual talks are requested to email an Abstract of between 300-600 words to UrbinoAbstracts@codebiology.org
Abstracts should be sent as one-page files written in standard formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2016. Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
http://www.codebiology.org/conferences_menu.html
For any question, please contact the Conference Organizer at  almo.farina@uniurb.it
Looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in Urbino.
