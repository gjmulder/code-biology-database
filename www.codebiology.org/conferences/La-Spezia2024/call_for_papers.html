Call for Papers

The Tenth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in La Spezia (Italy) 11 – 15 June 2024, under the auspices of the International Society of Code Biology and of the Accademia di Scienze e Lettere ‘Giovanni Capellini’.
The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions and the General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

The excursion of the conference will be a visit to the ‘Cinque Terre’, one of the most famous sea resorts in Italy, under the guidance and the support of the La Spezia City Hall.

People who wish to deliver a talk are invited to email an Abstract of between 250-500 words to laspezia-abstracts@codebiology.org

Abstracts should be written in formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2024
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
https://www.codebiology.org/conferences/La-Spezia2024/

For any question, please contact the local organizer:

Looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in La Spezia!
