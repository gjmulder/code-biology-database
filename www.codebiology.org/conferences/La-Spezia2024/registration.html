Registration


The Registration Fee is € 100 and can be paid in cash on arrival at the reception desk.
The Registration Fee covers the following expenses:

(1) Welcome party
(2) Coffee-breaks
(3) Conference Excursion
(4) Conference Dinner
(5) Conference material (booklets, badges, certificates)
(6) Rent of the Conference Hall

A conference participation certificate will be issued at the registration desk.


NB – People can register and take part in the Conference even if they are not presenting a communication.
