Call for Papers

The International Conferences in Code Biology aim at bringing together scholars and researchers who are interested in the study of all codes of life, from the genetic code to the codes of culture.
The First International Conference in Code Biology will be held in Paris, from 20 to 24 May 2014, under the auspices of the Université Paris Descartes and the International Society of Code Biology.
In addition to individual talks, the Conference will host a Round-Table discussion on the relationship of Code Biology with other disciplines.
[1] People who wish to deliver individual talks are requested to email an Abstract of between 300-600 words to info@codebiology.org
[2] People who wish to take part in the Round-Table discussion should email a brief Summary (less than 300 words) of the issue they want to illustrate to the same address info@codebiology.org
Abstracts and Summaries should be sent as one-page files written in standard formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF, please).
The deadline for both Abstract and Summary submissions is 15 January 2014.
Earlier submission is highly recommended.
More details are available in the Conference homepage www.codebiology.org/conferences/home.html
Looking forward to seeing you in Paris.
