Hotels

Rooms for the conference participants have been reserved by the organizers in the Hotel Írottkő, which is the main Hotel in Kőszeg and lies in the town center, very close to the conference venue.
Accommodation for one person costs 12,500 HUF (approx. 42 Euro) a night, including breakfast.
When reserving, please mention CodeBiology2017 for the special conference rate to apply.
