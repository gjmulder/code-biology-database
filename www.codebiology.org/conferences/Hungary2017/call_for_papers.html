Call for Papers

The International Conferences in Code Biology aim at bringing together scholars and researchers who are interested in the study of all codes of life, from the genetic code to the codes of culture. 

The Fourth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Kőszeg (Hungary), from 22 to 26 May 2017, under the auspices of the Istitute of Advanced Studies Kőszeg (iASK) and the International Society of Code Biology.
The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver individual talks are invited to email an Abstract of between 300-600 words to
Koszeg-Abstracts@codebiology.org
Abstracts should be sent as one-page files written in formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).

The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 February 2017.
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
http://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Hungary2017

For any question, please contact the Conference Organizers:
Eörs Szathmáry at szathmary.eors@gmail.com
or Ádám Kun at kunadam@elte.hu

Looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in Kőszeg
