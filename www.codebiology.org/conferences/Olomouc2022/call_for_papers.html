Call for Papers

The Eighth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Olomouc (Czech Republic) 7 – 11 June 2022, under the auspices of the International Society of Code Biology  and of the Department of General Linguistics of the Palacký University in Olomouc. The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver a talk are invited to email an Abstract of between 250-500 words to Olomouc-abstracts@codebiology.org

Abstracts should be written in formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2022
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
http://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Olomouc2022/

We are looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in Olomouc!
