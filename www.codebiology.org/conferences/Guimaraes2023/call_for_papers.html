Call for Papers

The Ninth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Guimarães  (Portugal)  6 – 10 June 2023, under the auspices of the International Society of Code Biology  and of the University of Minho.
The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver a talk are invited to email an Abstract of between 250-500 words to guimaraes-abstracts@codebiology.org

Abstracts should be written in formats such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2023
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
https://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Guimaraes2023/

For any question, please contact the local organizer:
