Call for Papers

The International Conferences in Code Biology aim at bringing together scholars and researchers who are interested in the study of all codes of life, from the genetic code to the codes of culture.

The Second International Conference in Code Biology will be held in Jena, from 16 to 20 June 2015, under the auspices of the Friend Shiller University and the International Society of Code Biology.

In addition to individual talks, the Conference will host Poster sessions, a Roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.


[1] Preliminary Conference Schedule:

Tu   16.06.2015 Arrival day 19.00 Welcome Reception for the Conference
We 17.06.2015 9.00-18.00 Main Conference, talks, posters.
Th   18.06.2015 9.00-18.00 Main Conference, talks, posters.
Fr   19.06.2015 9.00-18.00 Main Conference, talks, posters, Roundtable, General Assembly.
Sa   20.06.2015 Departure day (or Excursion on request).

People who wish to give individual talks at the Conference are requested to submit an Abstract of between 300-600 words either via EasyChair, or by email to jenaconference@codebiology.org

The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 February 2015.

Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage http://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Jena2015/


The Conference will be preceded by two Tutorial days on 15-16 June 2015

[3] The Jena Summer School in Code Biology

It is the combination Tutorials+Conference. Those who take part in these two events will receive a certificate of participation in the Jena Summer School in Code Biology (14-20 June 2015).
