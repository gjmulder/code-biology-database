Hotels

Please note that there are only few big hotels in Jena’s centre, which are usually fully booked during the conference season. Numerous smaller hotels, B&Bs and hostels are available, some of which are listed below. We recommend you to book well in advance. For some of you Airbnb might be an option

(https://www.airbnb.de/s/Jena?source=bb&ss_id=9p4jgjnt)
