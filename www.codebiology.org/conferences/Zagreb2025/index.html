Eleventh International Conference in Code Biology

Zagreb (Croatia), 10 – 14 June 2025

The conference will take place in the Faculty of Philosophy and Religious studies of the University of Zagreb. The mission of the Faculty is multilayered: to cultivate and improve philosophy and religious studies programs; to deeper understanding of man and his life; to promote philosophical and religious reflection; to develop a dialogue with the modern world and society.

  • The Faculty is close to the town centre of Zagreb
  • Within the main building there are 3 Lecture Halls, Seminar and Meeting rooms, Library and Cafè
  • The participants will have free access to all Faculty facilites
  • A Lecture Hall that can easily accommodate 50 people will be at our disposal during the meeting, the opening ceremony, the lectures and the General Assembly
  • Coffe breaks will take place at the Faculty Cafè next to the Lecture Hall
