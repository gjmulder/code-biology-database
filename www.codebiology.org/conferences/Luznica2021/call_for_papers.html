Call for Papers

The Seventh International Conference in Code Biology, originally planned in June 2020, has been postponed because of the covid pandemic and will take place in Lužnica (Croatia) on 31 August – 4 September 2021.

The conference is organized under the auspices of the University of Zagreb, Faculty of Pharmacy and Biochemistry, and of the International Society of Code Biology. It will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver a talk are invited to email an Abstract of between 200 - 500 words to Luznica-abstracts@codebiology.org

Abstracts should be one-page files and should be written in .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).
The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 April 2021

NB – Those who have already submitted an Abstract for the 2020 Conference are kindly invited to submit it again in order to confirm that they intend to take part in the 2021 Conference

More details are available in the Conference homepage
http://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Luznica2021/

For any question concerning the conference please contact the local organizer, Nikola Štambuk, at  stambuk@irb.hr

We are looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to welcoming you in Lužnica!

 

Nikola Štambuk, Paško Konjevoda and Marcello Barbieri

Conference Organizers
