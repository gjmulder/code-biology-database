Re-programming of the 7th International Code Biology Conference

Dear Colleagues,

The Seventh International Conference in Code Biology was postponed to June 2021 on the expectation that by that time a safe meeting would be possible, but the evolution of the pandemic has shown that even that date is at risk. This is because many people may not be able to be vaccinated before June 2021, whereas it is virtually certain that they can be vaccinated before September 2021.

This is why the organizing committee has decided that the next conference in Code Biology is delayed by 3 months and will take place in Lužnica (Croatia) in the period  31 August – 4 September 2021

The deadline of the Abstracts has also been postponed by 3 months and is now  15 April 2021

We can only rely on your determination to meet again despite the difficulties caused by the pandemic and very much look forward to welcoming you in Lužnica next September!

Your Conference Organizers
Nikola Štambuk, Paško Konjevoda and Marcello Barbieri
