Full-board Accommodation

We have been able to find an agreement with the Spiritual-Educational Center Mary’s Court, Lužnica whereby the conference participants can have full board accommodation (single room, breakfast, lunch and dinner) for the 4 days of the conference at the following terms:

• 53 single rooms with full-board accommodation at € 85 Euro per person per day  
• 7 comfort double rooms with full-board accommodation at € 85 Euro per person per day



Participants are asked to make early reservations. Please send an email to the local organizers - Nikola Štambuk (stambuk@irb.hr) and Paško Konjevoda (pkonjev@irb.hr) - saying that you will participate in the conference and stating day of arrival and day of departure.

There are no deposit nor credit card details needed for the booking. However, in the event that you must cancel your reservation, you are kindly requested to let the organizer know at least three days before the conference.
