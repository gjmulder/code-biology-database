Registration



The Registration Fee is € 70 and can be paid in cash on arrival at the reception desk.


The Registration Fee covers the following expenses:

(a) Rent of the Conference Hall
(b) Coffee-breaks
(c) Conference material (booklets, badges, certificates, etc.)
(d) Excursion to Krapina Neanderthal Museum


The treasurer of the International Society of Code Biology (Lutz Strüngmann) will issue the receipts and will present a financial report during the General Assembly at the end of the Conference.

NB – People can register and take part in the Conference even if they are not presenting a communication.
