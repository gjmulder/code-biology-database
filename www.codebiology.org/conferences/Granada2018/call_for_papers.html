Call for Papers

The International Conferences in Code Biology aim at bringing together scholars and researchers who are interested in the study of all codes of life, from the genetic code to the codes of culture.

The Fifth International Conference in Code Biology will take place in Granada (Spain) from 5 to 9 June 2018. The Conference will host individual talks, poster sessions, a roundtable discussion and the Annual General Assembly of the Code Biology Society.

People who wish to deliver individual talks are invited to email an Abstract of between 200-500 words to Granada-Abstracts@codebiology.org
Abstracts should be sent as one-page files written in a format such as .doc or .rtf (no PDF please).

The deadline for Abstract submission is 15 January 2018.
Earlier submission is highly recommended.

More details are available in the Conference homepage
http://www.codebiology.org/conferences/Granada2018/

Looking forward to receiving your Abstract and to seeing you in Granada
