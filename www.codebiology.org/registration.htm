Governing Board

The International Society of Code Biology (ISCB) has been officially constituted in Ferrara (Italy) before notary
Chiara Maria Florio on 28 November 2012. The present Governing Board is formed by the following members:

1 João Carlos Major president International Academy of Analytical Psychology, Portugal
2 Elena Fimmel vice-president Mannheim University of Applied Sciences, Germany
3 Lutz Strüngmann media Mannheim University of Applied Sciences, Germany
4 Nikola Štambuk genetic code Ruđer Bošković Institute, Zagreb, Croatia
5 Robert Prinz database Rechenkraft.net, Marburg, Germany
6 Chris Ottolenghi Université Paris Descartes, France
7 Poria Azadi University of Tehran, Iran
8 Almo Farina University of Urbino, Italy
9 Lukáš Zámečník Palacký University, Olomouc, Czech Republic
10 Camilla Robuschi University of Torino, Italy
11 Candice Shelby University of Colorado Denver, USA
12 Marcella Faria Instituto Butantan, São Paulo, Brazil
13 Omar Paredes University of Guadalajara, Mexico
14 Donald Favareau National University of Singapore
15 Victoria Alexander Dactyl Foundation, New York, USA

Founder and honorary president:      Marcello Barbieri, University of Ferrara, Italy
