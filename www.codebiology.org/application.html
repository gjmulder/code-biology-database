Application for Membership

Applications for membership are welcome from scholars of various disciplines, including biology, philosophy, semiotics, information theory, linguistics, anthropology and ecology.
If you wish to become a member of the International Society of Code Biology, please send an email, together with a short curriculum, to the founder of the Society (marcello.barbieri@unife.it) or to its president (nikolastambuk@gmail.com).
The membership is free of charge. Members are requested to pay a registration fee only when they attend a conference of the society.
